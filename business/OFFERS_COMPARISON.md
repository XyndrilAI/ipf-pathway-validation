## Decision Guide

**Choose Cohort Expansion if:**
- You want to strengthen a publication/grant with more cohorts
- You don't need the framework itself, just the results
- Timeline: You can wait 3 weeks

**Choose Lab Adoption if:**
- You want ongoing capability (not one-off analysis)
- You have your own unpublished data to validate
- You want your team trained to use the framework

**Choose Custom Validation if:**
- You have a specific signature/hypothesis to validate
- You want independent, unbiased assessment
- You need results quickly (10-14 days)
